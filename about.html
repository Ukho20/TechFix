About Us

Who we are and why we’re passionate about technology.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, TechFix Solutions was created with one mission in mind: to make technology simple, reliable, and accessible for everyone. From humble beginnings fixing laptops for friends, we’ve grown into a trusted provider of IT support for homes and businesses.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver fast, affordable, and professional IT solutions that empower individuals and organizations to work smarter. Whether it’s repairing devices, setting up networks, or creating custom software, we’re here to help.

Our Vision

To become the go-to IT services provider in South Africa and beyond, known for reliability, trust, and innovation.
